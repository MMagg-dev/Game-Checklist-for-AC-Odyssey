# AC Odyssey - Checklist for Comptionists

An unofficial checklist for Assassin’s Creed Odyssey. Explore Greece, Elysium, Underworld, and Atlantis and make sure you don’t miss out on any quest, tomb, viewpoint, or collectible in the game.

[![Google Play](https://play.google.com/intl/en_us/badges/images/badge_new.png)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=me.mmagg.aco_checklist)


### Checklists Available
* Main Storyline quests
* Side quests
* Ainigmata Ostraka, tombs and viewpoints
* Cult of Kosmos
* Mythical creatures and legendary beasts
* Legendary armor sets and weapons
* Legendary Phobos skins, crew themes, figureheads, lieutenants, and ship designs
* Achievements (secret, optional and all DLCs)
* Lost Tales of Greece DLC quests
* Legacy of the First Blade DLC quests
* Order of the Ancients (from Legacy of the First Blade DLC)
* Fate of Atlantis DLC quests
* Fate of Atlantis DLC collectibles and activities – Ainigmata Ostraka, Marble Maiden Tributes, Perception of Hermes, The Fallen, tombs, and viewpoints


### App Features
* Option to hide completed items
* Some lists have sorting available so its easy for you to find items
* Option to show or hide spoilers for members of Cult of Kosmos and the Order of the Ancients
* Option to show or hide secret achievements
* Option to reset all tracked items
* Dark theme for supported devices (all devices running Android 10 and other devices where you can set dark theme or night mode from main device settings)
